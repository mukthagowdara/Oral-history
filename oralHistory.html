The Matthews

Ardelle Matthews and Robert Matthews after a distinguished career in the education field continue to serve many organizations such as Jackie Robinson, YMCA Board, RADY Children's Hospital, Team Ghana Sister city and more.

Carell Reavis

Retired Gunnery Sgt. Reavis was among the first 20,000 African American men who enlisted in the Marines and was sent to the segregated Montford Point Camp located in Camp Lejeune, North Carolina.

Caroll Waymon

Appointed head of San Diego's first human relations agency, Dr. Carrol Waymon, was charged with educating the public about racial tension and identifying racial injustice through community dialogue.
